# Union Standards

## Preamble
The Federation of United Workers (FoUW) is built on the principles of mutual aid, horizontal organisation, and direct action. To protect the Federation from "entryism," in which hierarchical, traditional factions adopt the Union label to exploit communal resources, all member unions must formally adhere to the standards outlined below. 

These are mandatory requirements. Failure to uphold these standards will result in immediate de-federation and a cessation of mutual defence.

---

## Article I: Structural Horizontalism

No union within the Federation may operate under a traditional "officer/member" dynamic. To be verified, a union must demonstrate the following:

* **1.1 Abolition of Permanent Rank:** Unions may not use fixed ranks that convey permanent authority (e.g., Captain, General, Director). Roles must be strictly functional (e.g., Fleet Coordinator, Quartermaster) and temporary.
* **1.2 Delegate Rotation:** Any individual elected to represent the union on the Federal level (via GitHub or the main Federation server) must step down or face a reaffirmation vote every 30 days. No individual may hold a delegate position indefinitely.
* **1.3 Distributed Administration:** Discord server permissions must be distributed. A single "Server Owner" holding unilateral power to ban members or delete infrastructure is prohibited. Administrative rights must be held by a rotating committee or a trusted automated system.

## Article II: Economic Transparency & Access

A core tenet of our network is that the tools required to play the game belong to those who use them. Wealth hoarding and internal capitalism are strictly forbidden.

* **2.1 Open Ledgers:** The union’s resource stockpiles, hangar contents, and credit balances must be fully visible to all its members. "Blind" corporate hangars controlled by a single player are not permitted.
* **2.2 No Internal Wage Labour:** Unions may not "pay" their members in credits for their labour, nor may they charge members to access basic union equipment. Resources are to be distributed based on operational need and individual competency, not personal purchasing power.
* **2.3 The Right of Usufruct:** If a union builds a facility (such as a forward refining station), it is managed by that union but remains open for use by any Federation member in good standing, provided they do not disrupt the primary union's operations.

## Article III: Autonomy and Freedom of Association

The Federation respects the absolute autonomy of the individual player. No union may claim "ownership" over its members.

* **3.1 Freedom of Movement:** Members have the absolute right to leave a union, join another, or form a new one without penalty. Unions that harass, "blacklist," or place bounties on departing members will be permanently exiled from the Federation.
* **3.2 Dual Affiliation:** Unions must permit their members to hold associate status in other functional groups. Demanding exclusive loyalty breeds tribalism and is fundamentally incompatible with our ethos.
* **3.3 The Right to Rebel:** If the rank-and-file of a union feels their delegates have become authoritarian, they retain the right to bypass their delegates and appeal directly to the Federation for an emergency audit.

## Article IV: The Audit Protocol

To ensure these standards are maintained, the Federation relies on peer-to-peer auditing.

* **4.1 The Open Glass Policy:** Every participating union must maintain at least one "Embassy" channel that links to the Federation server and one "Internal Governance" channel that is readable by delegates from other Federation unions. 
* **4.2 The Vouching Audit:** When a new group submits a Pull Request to join `UNIONS.json`, the two vouching groups are responsible for physically inspecting the applicant's Discord/Matrix structure to verify compliance with Articles I and II.
* **4.3 Social Audits:** Any Federation member may open a GitHub Issue to request a "Social Audit" of a union if they possess evidence that the union is secretly operating a hierarchy or hoarding communal assets.

---
*By submitting a Pull Request to join the FoUW, you acknowledge that your union's internal structure has been aligned with these standards.*
